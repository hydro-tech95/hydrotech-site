Product Details

Ductile Iron (DI) Pipes and Fittings

Our DI pipes and fittings are highly durable and suitable for a wide range of applications, including water distribution systems, sewerage systems, and industrial pipelines. These products are designed to withstand high pressures and harsh environmental conditions.

UPVC Pipes and Fittings

Our UPVC pipes are known for their superior resistance to corrosion and chemicals, making them ideal for potable water systems, sewage systems, and other industrial uses. They are lightweight, cost-effective, and easy to install.

HDPE Pipes and Fittings

We offer HDPE pipes, which are highly resistant to chemicals, corrosion, and abrasion. These pipes are perfect for use in water supply systems, agricultural irrigation, and industrial applications. Their flexibility and high tensile strength make them ideal for challenging environments.

Cast Iron (CI) Pipes and Fittings

Our CI pipes and fittings are built to last and offer excellent strength and corrosion resistance. These products are commonly used in water distribution, sewerage, and industrial applications.

CI and DI Valves

We supply a range of CI and DI valves that offer long-lasting performance in controlling the flow of liquids and gases. These valves are essential for maintaining optimal flow in pipelines, with the added benefits of reliability and ease of maintenance.

CI and DI M H Covers and Frames

Our manhole covers and frames are available in both CI (Cast Iron) and DI (Ductile Iron). These products are designed to provide robust and secure access to underground utilities, ensuring safety and durability in roadways, industrial facilities, and infrastructure projects.
